Core Brand Identity
BenneBae is a quick-service South Indian restaurant chain specializing in Davangere-style cuisine from Karnataka. The name combines "Benne" (Kannada for Butter) and "Bae" (modern slang for Before Anyone Else / term of endearment), emphasizing their star ingredient: white butter.

Aesthetic: The brand uses a distinct Emerald Green & White color palette. The interiors blend modern minimalism with traditional rustic elements, such as antique wooden doors.
​

Tagline: "We are serving people with all our heart, We are grateful that God has chosen us for this purpose".
